Space Game

An unfinished first person shooter with pixelated graphics style straight from the 90s. The game is no longer in development, but I might return to it sometimes. Here's some story, that wasn't implemented:
You are an astronaut who arrives on an orbit station "121-Alpha". The station is controlled by a robot (who's name I haven't made yet), who acts like a regular autopilot, but in reality, he controlls evil robots who infested the entire base. It is your duty to kill all the robots, and then face the final boss of the game (the main evil robot).
The game was meant to be played in singleplayer or multiplayer.
