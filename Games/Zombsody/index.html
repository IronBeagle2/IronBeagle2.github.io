Zombsody 2D

Zombsody is a 2D top-down shooter in which you play as one of the only survivors of a zombie apocalypse. Your goal is to kill all the zombies standing in your way, and get into the city that your family lives in. Hopefully you'll find them alive hiding somewhere in a basement.
You can also try the Arena game mode, in which you select a difficulty, and try to survive as long as possible on an island.

Download

The game is still heavily under development, but it's slowly but surely coming along. Most of the mechanics are already in.
You can download a demo version of the game here.
Keep in mind that it is still not finished, so there will be bugs. Please report them on my Discord Server.
