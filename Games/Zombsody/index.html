Zombsody 2D

Zombsody is a 2D top-down shooter where you play as a survivor of a zombie apocalypse. Your goal is to kill all zombies in a city that your family lives in, and hopefully find them alive hiding in a basement. The game is still heavily under development, but it's slowly but surely coming along. Most of the mechanics are already in. My current focus is designing levels. After that I need to add animations.

Download

You can download a development version of the game here. Keep in mind that it is still not finished, thus there might be bugs. Please report them on my Discord Server.

Trivia

Zombsody 2D was originally made in around 5 hours as a personal challenge. You can play the bad original version here. Special Thanks goes to: NostalgiaModder for creating most of the textures, SteleCat for coming up with the game's name, and the rest of Club Classic Revived for beta testing and giving me feedback on the game.
